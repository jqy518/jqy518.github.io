China is an prosperous and glory country, it has grown a lot since the moment when history was recorded; however, the traditional festivals and foods are not disappearing but preserved. Chinese culture is one of the world’s oldest civilization, it includes the rich arts, special cuisines, and the variety of festivals that belong to China only. The Chinese cuisines taste extremely delicate and create unique style own by Chinese. The traditional Chinese foods has existed for hundreds or even thousands of years, and the Chinese festivals play a major role in Chinese culture because they have heavily influenced it. There are not only lively festival such as dragon boat festival where you can hear yelling and musics but also creepy festivals such as Yulan festival where you burn cloths and dancing for the ghosts. There are also festivals that involve worships of animals such as the ridiculous Niu Wang Dan festival where people put their kids beside the powerful cows wishing the cows would make them strong, and beatific Renri festivals where people giving each other the beautiful flower and wearing Rensheng for showing the beauty of humanity. Other people might think those practice are ridiculous and unworthy to do it, but no one feels shame by doing embarrassing actions because the festivals have already became part of their body organs. Furthermore, Guangdong has delicious food that will draw your attention. Guangdong has foods such as Chinese egg roll, wonton noodle, clay pot rice, and steam vermicelli roll. Firstly, the Chinese egg roll, it is healthy, tasty and doesn’t add any preservative. The egg roll has a lot of recipes. People can add anything they like in it, it is just based on the taste people like to eat. Secondly, the wonton noodle. It has a long history and there is a very amusing legend about the Xiongnus. It is light tasted and plenty of foodies will come to eat it. Also, the clay pot rice, is has a special taste of the history of China. The sausage and the chicken give the soul of this food. The sausage give the food a little bit sweetness. Lastly, the steam vermicelli roll, the roll is very crafty and the sauce of it is special, it adds soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar.

HISTORY ANALYSIS

Guangdong is a rapidly developing first tier province. Because of that, many people only sees it as a magnificent province and a place for countless young people to pursue their dreams. However, few people in China, or even in Guangdong, do not know that there are many traditional food and festivals with a long history in Guangdong. The foods that originated in guangdong were called Cantonese cuisine, for example steamed vermicelli roll, spring roll, wonton noodles, and Steamed Rice in Clay Pot. There are people who’s interested in guangdong and wants to get a job here, wants to live here, wants to have a trip here...... The steamed vermicelli roll is smooth and had its unique salty sauce, it’s made during the Tangd dynasty, which is more than 1000 years ago, lots of Nobel class liked it, and it’s named by the emperor. The egg roll is very famous in China, you can see people selling them everywhere. The wonton noodle Wonton noodles from Guangdong Province is rated as one of the top ten famous noodles in China. Clay pot steamed rice has a history of more than 2000 years. The festival of guangdong were also important, local people celebrate the birth of Niuwang during niuwangdan to show the respect to the cow and the earth, they worship ghosts and underworld gods not to bring them bad luck during the yulan festival, they celebrate the day Nuwa mades human by clay during renri festival, people made small paper human to put it on their head, they made flower decorations and give them to other people. they have dragon boat festival to anniversary the poet quyuan’s death. All these were originated and famous in guangdong, they should be know by other people, We should celebrate these festivals together, not let it be known by a small number of people.
